About Me

Hi, I am Apurv Samant. I work as a Web Developer at Indoc Research where my focus is on developing responsive websites, dashboards and data web portals, building product prototypes and Material UI designs. I graduated with a MSc in Computer Science from University of Plymouth, UK. I spent the past 8 years working from a Junior Web Developer to UI/UX Specialist in London (England), Mumbai (India) and Toronto (Canada).

When not coding/designing, I enjoy traveling and exploring places as well as watching and supporting my favourite football club Manchester United and other sports in general.
